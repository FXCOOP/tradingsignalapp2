3 Enable Health Check Path

This ensures Render keeps your service warm:

  1. Go to your Render dashboard: https://dashboard.render.com
  2. Click on tradingsignalapp
  3. Go to Settings tab
  4. Scroll to "Health Check" section
  5. Set Health Check Path to: / (your home page)
