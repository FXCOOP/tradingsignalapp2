‚ùì Common Questions

Q: What if I forget to set up a cron job?

A: Your signals API will still work, but you'll have to manually call it every day. Users will see old/stale signals, which hurts your credibility.

Q: Can I change the time to something other than 8 AM Dubai?

A: Yes! Just modify the cron expression. For example, 0 2 * * * = 6 AM Dubai, 0 6 * * * = 10 AM Dubai.

Q: What if the cron job fails one day?

A: EasyCron has email notifications. If a job fails, you'll get an alert. Plus, your API has fallback demo signals that will be returned if OpenAI is unavailable.

Q: Does this cost money?

A: $0.00 with EasyCron free tier. You get 20 free cron jobs, and you only need 1.

Q: Can I test it before it runs automatically?

A: Yes! EasyCron has a "Test Run" button. You can also test manually with curl (see Step 3 above).
