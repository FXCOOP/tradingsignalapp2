⚙️ Google Ads Campaign Setup

1️⃣ Create Search Campaign

  • Go to Google Ads
  • Click "+ New Campaign"
  • Goal: "Website traffic" or "Leads"
  • Campaign type: "Search"

2️⃣ Target Specific Countries

  • Locations: Select UAE, Egypt, Mexico, Argentina, Peru, South Africa, Kenya
  • Language: English, Arabic (for Egypt), Spanish (for Latin America)
  • IMPORTANT: Use "People in or regularly in your targeted locations" (not "People interested in")

3️⃣ Set Budget & Bidding

  • Daily budget: Start with $2-5/day per campaign
  • Bidding: "Maximize clicks" (to start)
  • After 100+ clicks, switch to "Maximize conversions"

4️⃣ Add Keywords

  • Use "Phrase match" for long-tail keywords: "how to trade forex"
  • Use [Exact match] for high-intent keywords: [best forex broker uae]
  • Add 10-15 keywords per campaign (don't overdo it)
  • IMPORTANT: Add negative keywords: -jobs, -course, -free course, -download

5️⃣ Create Compelling Ad Copy

  • Headline 1: Free Forex Signals & Trading Education
  • Headline 2: AI-Powered Market Insights 24/7
  • Headline 3: Get $80 Bonus with Exness (for UAE)
  • Description: Learn forex, gold, crypto trading with real-time signals. Join 10,000+ traders. Start with $10 deposit & get up to $80 bonus.
  • URL: https://tradeflow.blog

6️⃣ Install Conversion Tracking

  • Set up Google Ads conversion tracking on Exness clicks
  • Track when users click your Exness affiliate link
  • This helps Google optimize for conversions
