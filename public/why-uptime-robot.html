⚙️ How UptimeRobot Works

Simple 3-Step Process:

  1. Every 5 minutes: UptimeRobot sends a "ping" (HTTP request) to your website
  2. Your server responds: Render sees activity, so it stays awake
  3. Result: Your site never goes to sleep, always responds instantly
