1 Create Google Ads Account

What to do:

  1. Go to ads.google.com
  2. Click "Start Now" and sign in with your Google account
  3. Switch to Expert Mode (skip the guided setup)
  4. Add your billing information (credit card or bank account)
  5. Set currency to USD (cannot be changed later!)
  6. Set time zone to Egypt (GMT+2)
⚠️ Important: Once you set your currency and time zone, you CANNOT change them. Choose carefully!

7 Set Up Conversion Tracking

What to track: Partner clicks (Exness affiliate link clicks)

Option A: Google Tag (Simple)

1. In Google Ads, go to Tools → Conversions → New Conversion

2. Choose Website

3. Set these values:

Conversion Name:Partner_Outbound_Click
Category:Submit lead form
Value:$50 (estimated commission per action)
Count:One (only count first click per user)
Click-through window:30 days
Attribution model:Last click

4. Copy the conversion tracking code and add it to your website

Option B: Google Tag Manager (Advanced)

See your existing GTM setup in: GTM-to-Google-Ads-Complete-Guide.html

9 What to Do in the First Week

Day 1-2: Monitor Approval

  • Check if ads are approved (can take 24-48 hours)
  • If disapproved, read the reason and fix the issue
  • Most common: landing page language mismatch or missing disclaimer

Day 3-7: Optimize

  • Check Search Terms Report: Tools → Planning → Search Terms
  • Add new negative keywords for irrelevant searches
  • Check CTR: Should be 3%+ (if lower, improve ad copy)
  • Check Cost per Click: Should be $0.10-$0.25
  • Pause low-performing keywords with 0 clicks after 100 impressions

⚠️ Common Problems & Solutions

Problem: Ads Disapproved

Solution:

  • Check landing page is in Arabic (not English)
  • Add risk disclaimer: "التداول ينطوي على مخاطر. ليس نصيحة مالية"
  • Remove any "bonus" or "guaranteed profit" claims
  • Add privacy policy and contact information

Problem: Low CTR (under 2%)

Solution:

  • Improve ad headlines - make them more specific and appealing
  • Add more exact match keywords (better targeting)
  • Check search terms report and add negative keywords
  • Test different ad copy variations

Problem: High CPC (over $0.30)

Solution:

  • Lower max CPC bid limit to $0.25
  • Improve Quality Score (better landing page, higher CTR)
  • Use more long-tail keywords (less competitive)
  • Focus on educational keywords (cheaper than commercial ones)

Problem: No Conversions

Solution:

  • Test conversion tracking code (click partner link yourself)
  • Check landing page - is call-to-action clear?
  • Improve landing page design and trust signals
  • Give it time - need at least 100-200 clicks to see patterns
