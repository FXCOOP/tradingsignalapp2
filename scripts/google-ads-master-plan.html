A) Goals, Metrics, and Financial Model

1. ROI vs ROAS Definitions

Return on Investment (ROI)
ROI = (Revenue − Ad Spend) / Ad Spend × 100%

Example: $10,000 revenue − $1,000 ad spend = $9,000 profit
ROI = $9,000 / $1,000 = 9.0 or 900%
ROI Ratio = 9:1 (profit-to-spend)
Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)
ROAS = Revenue / Ad Spend

Example: $10,000 revenue / $1,000 ad spend = 10.0
For every $1 spent, you generate $10 in revenue

Relationship: ROAS = ROI + 1 (when expressed as ratio)
Clarification: "1:20 ROI" Goal

Interpretation 1 (Profit-based): For every $1 spent, you profit $20 → ROI = 20:1 → ROAS = 21.0

Interpretation 2 (Revenue-based): For every $1 spent, you generate $20 revenue → ROAS = 20.0 → ROI = 19:1

We will use Interpretation 2 (ROAS 20.0) as it's the standard industry interpretation and more conservative.

B) Compliance Hardening (Policy-Safe Rewrites)

1. Policy Risks Identified in Original Draft

⚠ HIGH-RISK ELEMENTS IDENTIFIED:
  1. "Get $80 Bonus with Exness" / "$60 مكافأة" / "Bono $XX"
    → Risk: Misrepresentation policy. Google prohibits ads that promote unrealistic bonuses or incentives that may mislead users about actual value/requirements.
    → Policy: Misrepresentation Policy
  2. "Free Forex Signals" / "احصل على إشارات مجانية"
    → Risk: Financial products policy. "Free signals" may imply investment advice without proper disclaimers; can trigger manual review.
    → Policy: Financial Products & Services
  3. "10,000+ traders" / "AI-Powered Trading Insights 24/7"
    → Risk: Unverifiable claims. Must be substantiated or removed.
    → Policy: Misrepresentation Policy
  4. Low daily budgets ($0.67–$1.67/day)
    → Risk: Insufficient for Google's learning algorithms; campaigns won't exit learning phase or gather meaningful data.
    → Recommendation: Minimum $10/day per campaign or consolidate into fewer campaigns.
  5. Maximize Clicks with no conversion tracking
    → Risk: Wastes budget on low-quality clicks; no optimization for actual business outcomes.
    → Recommendation: Set up conversion tracking immediately; use Maximize Conversions or tCPA.
